Spring Rebellion

A national declaration of civil disobedience.

Life on earth is in trouble.

Whole species are going extinct. People all over the world are suffering and dying. This isn’t a distant apocalypse. It will only get worse.

Governments are doing nothing. Businesses are doing nothing.

Who is going to save us?

It’s up to you. You can’t count on someone else to do this for you. Not us. Not Greta. It’s time to look inside yourself and take action.

We must rebel.

Starting on September XX, Extinction Rebellion is calling an International Rebellion. Together, we will peacefully occupy the centres of power and shut them down. We will demand that governments Tell the Truth about our Climate and Ecological Emergency. We will demand that they Act Now.

Leave your desk. Invite your boss. Walk out of school. Switch off the TV. Put down your phone.

Multiple actions in each state over the course of a week will organised with information provided ahead of time, contact information for the day, briefings on the day, and debriefs afterwards.

Made with love and rage

Extinction Rebellion (XR) is a do-it-together movement. All our design and artwork can be used non-commercially for the purpose of planet saving. This does not mean creating merchandise for fundraising or sending XR a percentage of your sales. We do not endorse or create any merchandise and we will pursue and prosecute anyone who does. The Extinction Symbol was designed in 2011 by street artist ESP, who loans XR usage on the same basis: www.extinctionsymbol.info
